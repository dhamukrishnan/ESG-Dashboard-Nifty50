# 📊 ESG Dashboard – NIFTY 50 Companies Analysis

## 🧭 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) analysis of NIFTY 50 companies using Power BI.

The dashboard is designed to evaluate company performance beyond traditional financial metrics by integrating sustainability, governance quality, and social responsibility into the analytical framework.

The objective of this project is to provide a structured ESG-focused perspective that supports sustainability-driven investment analysis and business evaluation.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem
Traditional company evaluation methods primarily focus on:
- Revenue
- Profitability
- Market capitalization

However, these metrics alone do not capture:
- Environmental exposure
- Governance-related risks
- Social responsibility performance
- Long-term sustainability

This project addresses that gap by building a centralized ESG analytics dashboard for NIFTY 50 companies.

---

# 🚀 Project Objectives
- Analyze ESG performance across NIFTY 50 companies
- Build a structured ESG scoring framework
- Compare companies across sectors
- Identify sustainability-related risk exposure
- Enable ESG-driven business insights

---

# 🧠 ESG Methodology Framework

## 🔹 Environmental Metrics
The environmental component evaluates:
- Carbon emissions
- Energy usage
- Environmental sustainability practices
- Waste and resource management

---

## 🔹 Social Metrics
The social component evaluates:
- Employee welfare
- Diversity & inclusion
- Workplace practices
- Community engagement

---

## 🔹 Governance Metrics
The governance component evaluates:
- Board structure
- Ethical business practices
- Transparency
- Corporate governance quality

---

## 🔹 ESG Score Calculation
The final ESG score is derived through aggregated Environmental, Social, and Governance indicators to provide a consolidated sustainability evaluation for each company.

# 📈 Key Insights Derived

## 🔍 ESG vs Market Leadership
The analysis highlights that large-cap market presence does not always correspond to strong ESG performance.

Several companies with strong market positions exhibit relatively weaker governance or environmental indicators.

---

## 🏭 Sector-Wise ESG Trends
- IT and FMCG sectors demonstrate relatively stable ESG performance
- Energy and industrial sectors show comparatively higher environmental risk exposure

---

## ⚠️ Governance & Sustainability Risks
Companies with weaker governance indicators may face:
- Regulatory concerns
- Operational instability
- Long-term reputational risk

---

## 💰 Investment Perspective
Strong ESG performers may indicate:
- Better long-term sustainability
- Higher governance stability
- Lower non-financial risk exposure

This project demonstrates how ESG analytics can support sustainability-focused investment evaluation.
